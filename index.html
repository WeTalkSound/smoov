How to participate.

  • Post the entry video of your group ( at least 4 members) performing to either I Dey There by Jujuboystar, Again by Wande Coal, Hallelujah by Funbi Music or Gentleman by Ric Hassani.
  • Post on your instagram page using the hashtag inharmonychallenge.
  • Tag the following instagram accounts @inharmonychallenge @smoovchapman @lifeataristokrat and make sure you follow all three pages.
  • Your entry must be a clear vocal recording without any background noise, instrument or production.
  • The challenge which is set to run/ for 12 weeks has 4 stages , The Call For Entry Stage, The knockout Stage, The Round of 10 Stage and the Clash of 4 Stage At the end of the call for entry duration, the top 20 group Contestants will be selected to move on to the knockout stage
Terms & Conditions



  • Delayed, misdirected or incomplete entries would not be recognized or accepted in this challenge.
  • Your entry must be a clear vocal recording without any background noise, instrument or production.
  • The Contestants are obliged to provide accurate, current, honest and complete information when participating in the challenge.
  • Aristokrat/Smoovchapman reserves the right to disqualify any group contestant if it has grounds to believe that the Contestant has breached any of the rules and/or terms and conditions applicable to the present challenge.
  • Call for entry deadline is the 28th of March 2021.
