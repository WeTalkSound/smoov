FAQs

  • Q: who can enter the challenge?
    A: Group of singers with at least 4 members.
  • Q: Am i required to sing with any instrument?
    A: No. your entry must be as clear as possible, we need to hear your voices without any instrument, production or background noise.
  • Q: How do I enter thé challenge?
    A: Post your entry video on Instagram or Twitter or Facebook or triller using the #inharmonychallenge and make sure to tag @inharmonychallenge, @smoovchapman and @lifeataristokrat.
  • Q: How many entry am I required to submit?
    A: 1 entry from the list of songs provided for the challenge.
  • Q: what songs am I required to perform to?
    A: You are required to perform to either 'I Dey There' by Jujuboystar, 'Again' by Wande Coal or 'Gentleman' by Ric Hassani.
  • Q: what is the prize for the challenge?
    A: winner goes home with N2,500,000 first runner up goes home with 1,250,000 and sending runner up goes home with 1,000,000
  • Q: when is the call for entry deadline?
    A: call for entry deadline is the 8th of March 2021.
